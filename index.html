    A planned self-sustaining floating research city.

    Project maintained by researchlaboratory Hosted on GitHub Pages — Theme by mattgraham

    Physics, Chemistry, Engineering, Biology, we've got it all.

    The Mid-Pacific Research Laboratory is a general-purpose research laboratory for just about any subject, from Physics to Biology to Electrical Engineering to Astronomy to (gasp) Oceanography.

    We're in the middle of the Pacific.

    There's nothing bothering you here. Every single building has an ocean view.

    Did I mention autonomous?

    There's almost no manual labor in this city. Everything is autonomous, including the Factory.

    That factory... what is it?

    If there's something you need made, create a Factory Definition File (.FDF file and send it off to the autonomous factory. Within a day, whatever you had made will be sent directly to your room, at no cost.

    Self-sustaining. Really.

    We have many eco-friendly mostly autonomous utility buildings that provide everything a research station in the middle of the ocean needs.

    • Hydro-atomic power
    • Desalination plant (Salt water to fresh water)
    • Non-autonomous fishing building (with rod lending!)
    • The Factory
    • Farm (where machines tend to plants and people tend to animals)
    • Autonomous mine
